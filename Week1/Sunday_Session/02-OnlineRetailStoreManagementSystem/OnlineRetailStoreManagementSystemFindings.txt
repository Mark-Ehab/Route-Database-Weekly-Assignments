Entity:
Product

Attributes:
ID (Unique)
name
description
unit price
stock quantity
date added

Relationships:
Every product belongs to a category, such as Electronics, Clothing, or Home Appliances.
Some products may belong to subcategories, which are also stored in the same table, allowing a category to have multiple subcategories (e.g., “Laptops” under “Electronics”)
a product may come from more than one supplier

ID (Unique)

Each order can contain many order items
Each order can have one or more shipments
